faculty of Science Eskişehir Technical University Faculty of Science has adopted the vision of being a pioneering faculty that produces basic information that will be the key to scientific innovations and provides education to the society by using all kinds of media. Faculty of Science; In addition to Physics, Chemistry and Biology, he gives education in the fields of Mathematics, which is the language of nature, and Statistics, which is the science of interpreting natural social science data using appropriate methods. Education in the faculty is carried out by making use of laboratory facilities equipped with advanced technology. Students also take vocational elective courses and social elective courses; thus, they are trained as individuals who are versatile, researcher qualified, self-confident and equipped with contemporary knowledge. The aim of the Faculty of Science is in laboratories and classrooms equipped with advanced technology; It trains rational, self-confident, questioning and researcher graduates in the fields of biology, physics, statistics, chemistry and mathematics. At least 30% of the courses are taught in English in the Statistics and Chemistry Departments of the faculty and there is a compulsory English Preparatory Class in these departments. In the Biology, Physics and Mathematics Departments, the English Preparatory Class is optional and lasts for one year. All departments of the faculty have been accredited by FEDEK for the second time for 5 years until September 30, 2023. The Faculty also has International Joint Undergraduate Programs (paid) run by the Biology and Chemistry Departments together with the Biology and Chemistry Departments of the State University of New York at Albany in the United States, and the language of instruction of these programs is English. There is an English Preparatory Class for these programs as well. Faculty of Science is affiliated to Eskişehir Technical University, which was established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425.
Faculty of Aeronautics and Astronautics Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences, a leading institution in aviation education; It offers undergraduate education in Aviation Electrical and Electronics, Air Traffic Control, Aviation Management, Pilotage and Airframe Engine Maintenance. The aim of the faculty is to bring qualified aviation personnel to the sector in the fields of air traffic control, aviation management, pilotage and aircraft maintenance in accordance with international aviation standards and air transport rules. In addition to flight training activities, the Faculty operates the International Eskişehir Hasan Polatkan Airport, where domestic and international flights are made. It carries out maintenance and repair activities for its fleet of 13 aircraft, including King Air C90GTi, Cessna 172 and TB20 types, which it uses in pilot training, at the SHY-145 approved Aircraft Maintenance Center. Simulation lessons are carried out using the FNPTII and MCC flight simulators, the Aerodrome Control (TWR) Simulator, which provides 360-degree views, the field and approach (ACC-APP) Air Traffic Control Radar Simulators, and the state-of-the-art synthetic training tools. In addition, students have the opportunity to receive practical training in their fields in 28 fully equipped laboratories and workshops. The Faculty was established in 1986 as "Civil Aviation Vocational School" in order to train qualified operational personnel in accordance with international standards for the Turkish Aviation Sector. With the opening of four-year undergraduate programs in 1992, the name of the school was changed to "Civil Aviation College". In 2012, it was restructured according to international higher education standards and the requirements of the global aviation industry, and renamed as "Faculty of Aviation and Space Sciences". Students are admitted to the Air Traffic Control Department within the faculty with a pre-registration and special talent exam, and to other departments with the central student selection and placement system exams (YKS).
architecture and design faculty Faculty of Architecture and Design was separated from Anadolu University and included in Eskişehir Technical University, which was established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30525. In the Faculty of Architecture and Design, there are Department of Architecture, Department of Interior Architecture, Department of Industrial Design and Department of Fashion and Textile Design.
engineering faculty It was established as Eskişehir State Engineering-Architecture Academy in 1970, and as a result of the restructuring of educational institutions in Eskişehir under the name Anadolu University in 1983, it continued its education and training by taking the name Anadolu University Faculty of Engineering-Architecture. With the Decree Law No. 496 dated 18.8.1993, the majority of the Faculty of Engineering and Architecture staff were transferred to Eskişehir Osmangazi University with their infrastructure, machinery, equipment and other equipment. The faculty, which still continues its education and training under the name of Engineering Faculty, started its education activities with three departments in the 1994-1995 academic year and continued its activities in different buildings on the Yunus Emre Campus until 1998. Since October 1998, it has started to serve in its own buildings on İki Eylül Campus. In 2012, the Department of Architecture within the Faculty was separated and transferred to the newly established Faculty of Architecture and Design, and the name of the Faculty was changed to “Faculty of Engineering”. The Faculty of Engineering is affiliated to Eskişehir Technical University, which was established with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018 and numbered 30425.
Sports Science Faculty The School of Physical Education and Sports, which was established with the Decree Law No. 496 dated August 18, 1993, was closed with the decision of the Council of Ministers (2013/5074) published in the Official Gazette dated July 24, 2013 and transformed into the Faculty of Sports Sciences. It was included in Eskişehir Technical University with the Law No. 7141 published in the Official Gazette dated May 18, 2018. To increase the quality of life of the people of the city, region, country and the world; to provide and disseminate universal knowledge through education, research and projects in sports sciences; to contribute to the growth of healthy people with sports and social applications; to support the society to make sports a way of life; Mediating the development of sportive abilities is among the main aims of the faculty. In the faculty, physical education and sports teachers, trainers, recreation specialists and sports managers are trained, who understand the interdisciplinary and multidisciplinary structure of sports science that will contribute to the development of sports throughout the country, are equipped in terms of professional knowledge and skills, can follow scientific and technological developments and apply these developments in their field. Practical lessons and sports activities in the faculty have a total indoor area of ​​66089 m² (multi-purpose – basketball, handball, volleyball – gym, artistic gymnastics hall, rhythmic gymnastics hall, badminton hall, table tennis hall, folk dances and wrestling hall, fencing hall, massage hall, fitness - fitness - hall, squash hall) in the halls, on the athletics track in accordance with IAAF standards and in the stadium with a grass football field with a seating capacity of 12527 people in accordance with the standards. In addition, a 5025-person indoor sports hall (750 m2 fitness room, saunas and doping control room are available in the hall).
